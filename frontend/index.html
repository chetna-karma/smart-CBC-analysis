Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions regarding our analyzer.

How does the CBC Analyzer calculate my Health Score? ▼

The Health Score starts at 100% and decreases depending on the severity and number of parameters that fall outside normal reference limits. High-risk deviations (such as critically low platelets or WBCs) deduct 30 points, while medium-risk deviations deduct 15 points.

What parameters are evaluated? ▼

We analyze the five core parameters of a Complete Blood Count: Hemoglobin (oxygen transport), White Blood Cell count (immune function), Platelet count (blood clotting), Red Blood Cell count (oxygen capacity), and Mean Corpuscular Volume (cell size and morphology).

Is this software a substitute for professional medical advice? ▼

No. This tool is designed to provide reference explanations, suggestions, and lifestyle suggestions based on physiological bounds. It is NOT a medical diagnosis and should always be reviewed alongside a licensed medical practitioner.
